reddit playbook by Diego Roshhardt

1. become a Active reddit user. 
becoming familiar with the platform 
warming up your account. (any post coming from newly created account will be automatically filter out by reddit )

2. create list of subraddits. 
the more and the better. 
use reddit ads tool (you dont need to pay). when you add a subraddit it will give you a suggestion . 

3. Killer organic post. 
provide value in your niche (educational, helpful, case study etc.. )
Plug your product. 
put your product link on reddit post (it has to be useful post ). add another product like as well. 
dont have a header like launching the product. 

4. post on multiple subreddit. 

5. repeat this process. 
post 2 to 3 times a week.
